By: Slaton H.B. No. 3850
 
 
 
A BILL TO BE ENTITLED
 
AN ACT
  relating to the construction of abortion statutes in relation to
  pregnant biological females who do not identify as a woman or
  female.
         BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:
         SECTION 1.  Section 311.036, Government Code, is amended by
  adding Subsection (d) to read as follows:
         (d)  A statute that regulates or prohibits abortion may not
  be construed to permit the performance or inducement of an abortion
  on a pregnant biological female who does not identify as a woman or
  female.
         SECTION 2.  Section 311.036(d), Government Code, as added by
  this Act, is intended to clarify rather than change existing law.
         SECTION 3.  This Act takes effect immediately if it receives
  a vote of two-thirds of all the members elected to each house, as
  provided by Section 39, Article III, Texas Constitution. If this
  Act does not receive the vote necessary for immediate effect, this
  Act takes effect September 1, 2023.
